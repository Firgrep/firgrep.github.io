I took my PhD in Philosophy in 2022 under the supervision of Professor Stephen Houlgate at the University of Warwick. The title of the thesis was Hegel's Critique of Determinism: Justifying Unfreedom as a Moment of Freedom. My main research areas are systematic philosophy, metaphysics, ontology, essence, freedom, determinism, and I maintain an otherwise broad interest in all the dimensions of intelligence and reason.


Besides teaching at university, I have created and taught courses online about Hegel, Collingwood and others. You can find them at the Halkyon Academy. I also tutor privately for those who look for additional guidance or challenge.


I am currently working on a project titled sPhil. More about that when it's ready.
