Economy

Sourcehood is a poetic journey through the labyrinthine echoes of inertia, anxiety, and failure. Divided into nine distinct parts, this poem delves into the intricate dance of inner life and outer surroundings, offering a poignant response to life's disheartening voices.


The story unfolds with an unnamed narrator ensnared in the suffocating confines of their existence, haunted by the relentless grip of harsh reality. In a serendipitous encounter, a guide materializes, and their fateful partnership sets the stage for an odyssey of self-discovery. Together, they embark on a quest to unearth the latent facets of their world.


Within these verses, mind, emotion, and habitat meld into the very essence of the soul, forming intricate organelles that reflect the profound question of transformation. Sourcehood intimately probes the reality of choice and the resolute decision to change one's trajectory. It's an exploration of the human spirit's resilience against the relentless tides of existence.
