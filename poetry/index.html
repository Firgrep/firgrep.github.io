"In the beginning was the word."


Poetry remains my longest running appointment. Even before it was put to the written word in some teenage dewy-anguished haze in 2006, was imagination a stalwart companion. My poetry ponders existential questions often expressed as inner journeys of the soul over a tapestry of reimagined myths and legends, laced with mysteries that evoke a sense of ambiguity, enigma or the unknown, all of which playfully border on the irrational, nonsensical and absurd.
